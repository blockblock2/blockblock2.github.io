About the Podcast

Ancient Mythteries dives deep into the mysteries, myths, and forgotten stories that shaped human civilization. From the halls of forgotten empires to the riddles of lost artifacts, we explore the questions that historians are still trying to answer.

Each episode is carefully researched and brought to life through interviews with experts, historians, and archaeologists who dedicate their lives to understanding our past.

Why listen?

  • Expert interviews with archaeologists and historians
  • Detailed research into lesser-known mysteries
  • Engaging storytelling that brings history to life
  • New episodes every two weeks
